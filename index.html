✦ About the Atelier ✦

Nocturna Atelier was born from the whisper of midnight muses and the pull of the macabre. Each painting is an invocation — acrylics, charcoal, and raw emotion blended to evoke dreams, shadows, and forgotten myths. Artist Jae Sheppard crafts every piece as a talisman for those who find beauty in darkness.

Our gallery is a sanctuary for collectors who seek the uncanny, the romantic grotesque, and the poetic grotesque. All works are original, signed, and ship worldwide in sustainable, ritual‑like packaging.
